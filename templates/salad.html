Salad

Salad is a miniature language used in place of English in StackOverflow’s Python chat room. It consists of about twenty words, all of which happen to be English names for vegetables, fruit, fungi or seeds.

History

The Salad language was created and introduced to the chat room on April 10, 2013 by room owner Jon Clements and Inbar Rose. The usage of the greeting quickly spread among regulars, and participants added additional words to the lexicon over time.

Metasyntactic Use

Much like the Smurfs, chat members will occasionally use “cabbage” as a placeholder word in circumstances where it would normally make no sense. For example, the motto of the room is “The productive programming cabbage”. Here, “cabbage” means “language”.
